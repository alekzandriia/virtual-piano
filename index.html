Keys

The number of keys on a keyboard or piano varies between models.

  • A standard piano has 88 keys, 52 white and 36 black.
  • A standard keyboard has 61 keys, 36 white and 25 black.

Regardless of the total number, boards are configured in a pattern of repeating seven white keys and five black keys.

Each iteration encompasses a full octave, and contains one key for each of the notes in the Western musical scale
